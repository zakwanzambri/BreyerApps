Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Profile

How do I update my profile information?
Go to Profile Settings from the profile menu in the header. You can update your personal information, contact details, and profile picture.
How do I change my password?
Navigate to Settings > Security > Change Password. Enter your current password and new password to update it.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Academic Features

How do I view my grades and transcripts?
Go to Dashboard > Academic Records. You can view current grades, semester transcripts, and overall GPA.
How do I register for courses?
Visit Academics > Course Registration during registration periods. Select your desired courses and submit your registration.
Where can I find my class schedule?
Your class schedule is available in Dashboard > Schedule. You can also export it to your calendar app.

Campus Services

How do I apply for accommodation?
Go to Services > Housing Application. Fill out the application form and submit required documents before the deadline.
Where can I find information about campus facilities?
Visit Services page for comprehensive information about dining, library, health center, and other campus facilities.
How do I report a technical issue?
Use the Contact Support option or email support@campushub.edu with details about the issue you're experiencing.
